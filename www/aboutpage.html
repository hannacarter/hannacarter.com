WHO IS HANNA CARTER

Hanna Carter is a student at Iowa State University majoring in Graphic Design. As a first year graphic design student she hopes to expand her knowledge on all aspects of graphic design as much as possible. She is also very interested in illustration and creative writing, and plans to pursue these interests alongside graphic design.

Aside from being an avid lover of all things graphic design, Hanna Carter is a bit of a geek. She loves music on level that can get a little bit over-zealous and random, but talking about music is one of her favorite conversations to have with people--provided they have good taste. She is also pretty big into film these days and hopes to incorporate more digital media into her career as a graphic designer. Beyond those things, Hanna likes to bake treats for her friends, pretend she knows how to do yoga well, and daydream about moving to San Francisco.

She hopes you enjoy her site!
